## Core Concepts & Frameworks
- **UCC Article 2** — US Uniform Commercial Code article governing sales of goods between merchants; supplies implied warranties, risk-of-loss rules, and remedy limitations. Source: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2
- **CISG** — United Nations Convention on Contracts for the International Sale of Goods; governs international sales contracts when both contracting states are parties. Source: https://uncitral.un.org/en/texts/salegoods/conventions/cisg
- **UNIDROIT Principles 2016** — International Institute for the Unification of Private Law principles for international commercial contracts; used for gap-filling and arbitration. Source: https://www.unidroit.org/instruments/commercial-contracts/unidroit-principles-2016
- **Restatement (Second) of Contracts** — American Law Institute synthesis of US common-law contract doctrine. Source: American Law Institute, 1981.
- **Risk-allocation analysis** — Indemnity, limitation of liability, warranties, IP indemnity, insurance, and remedy structure.
- **Plain-language drafting** — Clarity, defined terms, antecedent consistency, active voice, short sentences.
- **Boilerplate review** — Governing law, jurisdiction/venue, dispute resolution (litigation/arbitration), force majeure, assignment, notices, severability, entire agreement.
